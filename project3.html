Cook & Plant Game

This game is a 3D strategy simulation with a bird's-eye view, where players take on the role of a restaurant owner. The core gameplay revolves around managing and growing a restaurant business while carefully handling food production and waste management. Players must plan their planting cycles, cooking processes, and menu strategies efficiently to avoid exceeding a predefined waste limit. Each decision, from sourcing ingredients to preparing dishes, has an impact on the total waste generated. The challenge lies in maintaining a successful restaurant operation while staying environmentally responsible, encouraging players to think critically about sustainable resource use and operational efficiency

About the Game

Cook & Plant is a 3D top-down strategy simulation game where players take the role of a restaurant owner who must simultaneously manage cooking operations and farming activities. The core challenge lies in handling food waste efficiently—players must avoid exceeding the waste limit while maintaining smooth restaurant operations.

The game encourages sustainable practices by rewarding players for proper waste sorting and recycling. If the garbage bin reaches its limit, the game ends—making resource management a key mechanic. Players must think strategically, balance planting and cooking, and adapt to increasing difficulty across various levels.

Key Features

  • 🌱 Farming System: Grow various vegetables and ingredients needed for cooking.
  • 🍳 Cooking Recipes: Use harvested ingredients to prepare specific dishes based on active orders.
  • 🗑️ Waste Management: Sort and recycle organic and inorganic waste. Exceeding the waste bin limit will end the game.
  • 🔁 Recycling Mechanics: Recycle food scraps and trash for extra score and sustainability points.
  • 🕒 Time Challenge: Complete tasks within a time limit to progress through levels.
  • 🎮 Intuitive Controls: Simple keyboard movement and interaction suited for casual and strategy gamers.
  • 📈 Progressive Levels: Each level introduces new layouts, waste thresholds, and recipe combinations.
  • 📚 Tutorial Mode: Interactive guide to help new players understand game mechanics and controls.

This game was developed using the Unity Engine and C# as part of the Independent Study program under Skilvul in collaboration with Kampus Merdeka. It's available for free on Itch.io and targets PC users (Windows 8+).
